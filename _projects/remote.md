## Abstract

**ReMoTE** is a comprehensive benchmark designed to evaluate object motion transfer algorithms. We introduce two improved metrics — Masked Local Motion Fidelity and Video Motion Distance — that better correlate with human perception than existing motion fidelity scoring. The benchmark includes a diverse set of object categories and motion patterns, along with a standardized evaluation protocol to foster progress in motion transfer research.
